**For the UK:**

 Electronic cigarettes qualify under the [8543709099](https://www.gov.uk/trade-tariff/commodities/8543709099) commodity code.

 - Duty Rate: 3.7% (on imports outside the EU)
 - Sales Tax: 20%
 - No additional duties & taxes
